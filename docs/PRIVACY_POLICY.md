# Ancore Wallet Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-26

This Privacy Policy explains how Ancore Wallet ("Ancore", "we", "us", "our") handles information when you use the Ancore Wallet browser extension.

## 1. Scope

This policy applies to the Ancore Wallet extension and related support interactions. It does not apply to third-party websites, block explorers, or services you access through external links.

## 2. Information We Process

### 2.1 Information stored locally in your browser

The extension stores wallet application state in browser extension storage, such as:

- onboarding completion status
- lock/unlock status
- account and settings preferences
- session key configuration metadata

This data is stored locally on your device through extension storage APIs.

### 2.2 Information not required for basic extension operation

Ancore Wallet does not require account creation for basic extension usage and does not intentionally collect personal identity fields (for example, legal name, home address, or government ID) through the extension UI.

### 2.3 Support communications

If you contact support at `support@ancore.org`, we may process the information you provide (for example, email address and issue details) to respond and troubleshoot.

## 3. Permissions and Data Use

The extension currently requests:

- `storage`: to persist wallet state and settings on-device
- `activeTab`: to enable user-initiated wallet interactions with active tabs

We do not request broad host permissions such as `<all_urls>`.

## 4. How We Use Information

We use processed information to:

- provide extension functionality
- maintain wallet session and security controls
- improve product quality and reliability
- provide user support
- detect, prevent, and address misuse

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information.

We may share limited information only when necessary:

- with service providers assisting support/operations under confidentiality obligations
- if required by applicable law, regulation, legal process, or enforceable governmental request
- to protect rights, safety, and security of users and the service

## 6. Data Retention

Data stored by the extension remains on your device unless you remove it, reset extension state, or uninstall the extension. Support emails are retained for operational and compliance purposes for a reasonable period.

## 7. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 8. Your Choices

You can:

- remove extension data through browser extension settings
- uninstall the extension at any time
- contact support regarding privacy questions

## 9. Children's Privacy

Ancore Wallet is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 10. International Users

If you use the extension from outside your home jurisdiction, you acknowledge that information may be processed where support and infrastructure resources are located.

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date.

## 12. Contact

For privacy questions:

- Email: `support@ancore.org`
